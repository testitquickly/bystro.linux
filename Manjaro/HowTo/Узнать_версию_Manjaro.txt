====== Узнать версию Manjaro ======

[ @manjaro @version ]

''cat /etc/lsb-release''

Пример ответа:

	DISTRIB_ID="ManjaroLinux"
	DISTRIB_RELEASE="23.0.2"
	DISTRIB_CODENAME="Uranos"
	DISTRIB_DESCRIPTION="Manjaro Linux"
